Cute Culture in Japan

1. History:

Alisa Freedman and Toby Slade's book - Introducing Japanese Popular Culture: Provides historical so as political and economic context to Japanese popular culture.

i-D Meets: Tokyo's Genderless Youth: One of the subculture, related to Feminism.

3. Feminism and Sexism:

i-D Meets: Tokyo's Genderless Youth: One of the subculture, related to Feminism.
